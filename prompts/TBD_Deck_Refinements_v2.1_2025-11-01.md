---

## 🔥 High-Impact Headline Refinements

### **Slide 2: Problem**
**Before:** "Navis rebalances it."
**After:** "We're fixing it from the patient outward."

**Why It Works:**
- Active voice = confidence
- "Patient outward" = systemic redesign mindset
- Conveys momentum ("we're fixing" vs passive "rebalances")

**Impact:** Transforms passive observation → active execution

---

### **Slide 5: Market**
**Before:** "$230B" + "oncology market" (two separate lines)
**After:** "$230B oncology market—no one owns the patient."

**Why It Works:**
- Thesis instead of fact
- Strategic insight (market gap identification)
- Single powerful statement vs fragmented pieces

**Impact:** From market size stat → strategic opportunity

---

### **Slide 6: Why We Win**
**Before:** "We're the team hub."
**After:** "We're the command center."

**Why It Works:**
- "Command center" implies control + coordination
- Stronger positioning than "hub"
- Military/mission-critical connotation

**Impact:** From passive hub → active control point

---

### **Slide 7: Business Model**
**Before:** "10x faster."
**After:** "Weeks of waiting collapsed into hours."

**Why It Works:**
- Outcome > metric
- Emotional (waiting = pain)
- "Collapsed" = dramatic transformation verb

**Impact:** From abstract metric → visceral time savings

---

### **Slide 9: Team**
**Before:** "Built by survivors. For survivors." (two lines)
**After:** "Built by survivors who scaled systems."

**Why It Works:**
- Soul + credibility in one breath
- "Scaled systems" = execution track record
- More concise (removes "For survivors" redundancy)

**Impact:** From emotional appeal → credibility + emotion

---

### **Slide 11: Go-To-Market**
**Before:** "~$0 CAC"
**After:** "Advocacy groups are our distribution engine."

**Why It Works:**
- Insight about HOW, not just metric
- "Distribution engine" = systematic advantage
- Explains the $0 CAC mechanism

**Impact:** From metric → strategic insight

## 💡 Key Design Principles Applied

### 1. **Headline = Takeaway, Not Topic** (Jobs)
- ❌ Bad: "Market Size"
- ✅ Good: "$230B oncology market—no one owns the patient"

### 2. **Outcome > Metric** (Altman)
- ❌ Bad: "10x faster"
- ✅ Good: "Weeks of waiting collapsed into hours"

### 3. **Active Voice = Confidence** (Musk)
- ❌ Bad: "Navis rebalances it"
- ✅ Good: "We're fixing it from the patient outward"

### 4. **Command > Passive** (Military Precision)
- ❌ Bad: "We're the team hub"
- ✅ Good: "We're the command center"

### 5. **Mechanism > Metric** (Strategic Insight)
- ❌ Bad: "~$0 CAC"
- ✅ Good: "Advocacy groups are our distribution engine"
