## 🆕 New Slides Deep Dive

### Slide 12: GTM Phases (0→1→10)

**Purpose:** Answers "Can they execute?" with phased go-to-market roadmap

**Minimalist Version:**
- Headline: "0 → 1 → 10"
- Tagline: "From advocacy roots to platform scale."
- Visual: 3 cards (Advocacy → Labs → Enterprise)
- Word count: ~15 words

**Detailed Version:**
- Table with 5 columns: Phase, Channel, Example Partners, Core Metric, Milestone
- 3 rows:
  - **0→1 (2025-26):** Advocacy + Referral → 1K patients, $500K ARR, CAC ≈ $0
  - **1→5 (2026-27):** White-label Labs → 5K patients, $3M ARR, 5× volume
  - **5→10 (2027-29):** Enterprise/API → 100K+ patients, $25M ARR
- Tagline: "Paid ads optional accelerant — each phase compounds trust, data, and reach"

**Why It Works:**
- Shows **operational clarity** (not hand-wavy "we'll figure it out")
- Demonstrates **compounding mechanics** (each phase builds on previous)
- De-risks execution with **concrete milestones**
- Angels care about **0→1 execution**, this shows the path

**Sins Test:**
- ✅ Would a VC **feel** this? Yes - phased logic = confidence
- ✅ Can they summarize in one sentence? "Clear 3-phase scale path from advocacy to enterprise"

### Slide 13: Product Physics (Network Effects)

**Purpose:** Explains product moat through network effect mechanics

**Minimalist Version:**
- Headline: "Vault sharing drives networked trust."
- Tagline: "Every patient brings a care circle."
- Visual: Center vault + 4 surrounding collaborators (Caregiver, Doctor, Lab, Advocate)
- Metrics: "1 vault → 5 collaborators • 30% convert • CAC ↓ • Retention ↑"
- Word count: ~12 words

**Detailed Version:**
- Network diagram: Patient Vault (center) connected to 5 nodes (Caregiver, Oncologist, Lab, Advocate, Specialist)
- Each node labeled "Gets Navis link"
- Two insight boxes:
  - **The Network Effect:** 1 vault → 3-5 collaborators, 30% convert, trust loop → more uploads → more partners, Retention ↑ / CAC ↓
  - **Why This Compounds:** Collaborative virality > paid ads, each share expands the map, more data → better AI → stronger partnerships, network effects = defensible moat
- Tagline: "Each vault multiplies trust, engagement, and revenue — the platform grows exponentially, not linearly."

**Why It Works:**
- Explains **moat** (network effects = defensibility)
- Shows **growth mechanics** (collaborative virality > paid ads)
- Demonstrates **product-led growth** (users invite collaborators)
- Answers "Why can't competitors copy this?" → Network effects compound

**Sins Test:**
- ✅ Would a VC **feel** this? Yes - network effects = venture scale
- ✅ Can they summarize in one sentence? "Vault sharing creates viral network effects that compound"

### Slide 17: 5-Year Trajectory (Appendix)

**Purpose:** Reference for Q&A — shows venture scale potential ($50M ARR by 2029)

**Content:**
- Table with 5 columns: Year, Users, Revenue, GM, Key Driver
- 5 rows (2025-2029):
  - **2025:** 1K users, $0.4M revenue, 60% GM → 2 pilots live
  - **2026:** 5K users, $2M revenue, 65% GM → Advocacy flywheel
  - **2027:** 25K users, $8M revenue, 70% GM → White-label labs
  - **2028:** 100K users, $25M revenue, 72% GM → Enterprise deals
  - **2029:** 200K users, **$50M revenue**, 75% GM → Platform dominance

**Why It Works:**
- Shows **venture scale** ($50M ARR = VC-backable)

- **Mechanically driven** (not hockey stick fantasy)
- **Grounded in current unit economics** ($18K LTV, ~$0 CAC)

**Use Case:**
