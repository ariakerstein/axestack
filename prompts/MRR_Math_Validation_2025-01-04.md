2. Viral loops bring higher-engagement users
3. More referrals per patient as product matures

**Conservative estimate (Slide 10):**

- $10K → $50K MRR (5× growth)

## What's Causing the Confusion?

### **Root Cause: Two Different Growth Scenarios**

**Scenario A (Slide 10 - Conservative Volume Focus):**
- Prioritize patient acquisition over monetization
- Assume lots of free/low-engagement users
- Lower ARPU ($2-3/mo), higher volumes (5K → 25K → 100K)
- Result: Slower revenue growth, higher patient counts

**Scenario B (Slide 12 - Optimistic Monetization Focus):**
- Prioritize high-quality user acquisition + premium conversion
- Assume engaged users who convert and refer
- Higher ARPU ($3 → $5 → $6/mo), moderate volumes (5K → 10K → 25K)
- Result: Faster revenue growth, lower patient counts

**Problem:** Mixing scenarios across slides confuses investors.
