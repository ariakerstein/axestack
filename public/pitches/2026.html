Strategic Decision

A7

Why Listings, Not Trial Matching

We're the trusted front door, not the matching engine.

Dimension Trial Matching Partner Listings
Revenue / patient $150/yr (5% × $3K) $30/yr (audience access)
% patients monetized 5% (trial-eligible) 100% (all need services)
Competition Heavy (Massive Bio, TrialSpark) Less direct
What you're selling Data quality Patient attention + trust
Moat Weak (algorithms commoditize) Strong (trust compounds)
Compliance Complex (kickback-adjacent) Clean (visibility fee)
BD required Pharma relationships (hard) Labs, COEs, navigators (easier)

If we do Trial Matching...

We compete with Massive Bio on data quality. They have 160K patients + pharma relationships. We'd be fighting on their turf.

If we do Listings...

We own the patient relationship. Massive Bio becomes a partner (white-label), not a competitor. We're the front door they need.

Decision: Lead with Listings (patient trust = moat). Trial matching is upside via partnerships, not core business.

Qualified Audience Access

Partners pay for access to patients, not per action. Patient always chooses.

Our principle: Transparency. Listings are clearly disclosed. We never steer. Revenue doesn't depend on patient choices.
