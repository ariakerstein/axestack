Your data

Your records are yours.
Full stop.

We've been burned by health data being sold. So have you. Here's exactly what we do and don't do.

What we do

Store your records securely with HIPAA-compliant encryption. Use them to personalize your experience. Let you delete everything, anytime, permanently.

Anonymized research (with consent)

If you opt in, anonymized patterns from your data help improve the product and contribute to cancer research. You choose. You can opt out anytime.

What we never do

Sell your data. Share it with insurers. Share it with employers. Use it for advertising. Give it to pharma without your explicit consent.

The more you add, the smarter it gets.

Every record makes answers more specific to you. Every Combat run knows your biomarkers. Every question builds on the last. Upload one record — everything changes.
