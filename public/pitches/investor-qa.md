## 8. OBJECTIONS

**Liability?** Decision support only. Physician signs final order. Preparation layer, not prescription.

**Data access?** APIs (Epic/Cerner) + patient-authorized portal + fax-as-a-service.

**HIPAA/Privacy?** Patients own Vault. We don't sell data. Partners pay for connectivity.

**Scale with humans?** Current 1:20 → Target 1:1,000 (80% margin). AI handles 90% scut work; humans provide clinical signature + emotional reassurance.

## 9. THE SECRET

- **30-Day Dark Period** — Outcomes decided in month 1, but system is slowest. Highest-intent capture.
- **Caregivers are power users** — They're the "Human Fax Machines." Build for the folder-holder.
- **2nd opinions have systemic friction** — 84% never get one. Now possible to replicate expert access at scale.
- **Guidelines are suggestions** — 37% of plans change after expert review. Bandwidth crisis, not bad doctors.
